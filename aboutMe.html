About Me

Hello, I’m Domonkos Toth, a passionate student of law with a minor in data science and artificial intelligence. I am currently studying Politics, Psychology, Law, and Economics (PPLE) at the University of Amsterdam, with a major in law. My interests range from the intersection of technology and law, to cybersecurity, AI, and urban planning.

My work bridges the gap between legal frameworks and technological advances, with a focus on ethics and compliance in emerging tech industries. When I’m not immersed in research or development, you’ll find me enjoying running, exploring architecture, or reading both fiction and non-fiction.
